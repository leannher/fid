My Bio

When I was little, I loved to draw and build things. I was encouraged by the people in my life to improve. But there was a time when I stopped enjoying art and music. After a while I regained my appreciation for art and decided to pursue it in college. It was a difficult transition and at first, I was burnt out and tired of all the work. But slowly I’m becoming more used to receiving all this information. Now I’m still learning more and more about the things I love, and I’ll probably never stop finding things to learn about.
